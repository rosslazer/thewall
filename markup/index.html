New Explorations in Information Science

NEXIS is a student based, membership driven research center based in the Syracuse University iSchool. We are innovators supporting innovators, students supporting students, together building upon today's emerging technologies. NEXIS members freely work on independent projects to create novel innovations which advance the fields of information science, engineering, technology.

NEXIS is open to Syracuse University students from every discipline with the understanding that the best ideas are generated when people from diverse backgrounds and expertise work together. Interested in learning more? Check out our current happenings below, or reach out today!
